Privacy Policy

Last updated: October 7, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to iNNkie (“we”, “our”, “us”). We provide a URL shortening and link management service designed to help users manage and analyze links. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your personal data in accordance with data protection laws applicable in Africa, Europe, and North America.

2. Information We Collect

  • Personal Information: name, email address, and account credentials when you register or sign in (including via Google or other third-party authentication).
  • Usage Data: IP address, browser type, device identifiers, referring pages, clicks, location (country-level), and timestamps.
  • Cookies and Tracking: small data files used for authentication, analytics, and preferences. You can manage cookies through browser settings.

3. How We Use Information

We use collected data to operate and improve our services, analyze performance, personalize experiences, secure our systems, communicate updates, and comply with legal obligations. We never sell your personal data.

4. Legal Bases for Processing (EU/EEA & UK)

We process data based on: (a) your consent, (b) contractual necessity, (c) legitimate interests, and (d) legal obligations. Where consent applies, you may withdraw it anytime.

5. Data Transfers

Your data may be processed in servers located in Africa, the EU, or the United States. For international transfers, we rely on Standard Contractual Clauses or equivalent safeguards.

6. Data Retention

We retain personal information only as long as necessary for the purposes outlined or as required by law. Analytics linked to shortened URLs are retained while links remain active. Security logs are kept up to 12 months.

7. Your Rights

  • EEA/UK: Access, rectification, erasure, restriction, portability, objection, and complaint to supervisory authorities.
  • US (California): Access, deletion, and opt-out rights under CCPA/CPRA.
  • Africa: Rights under POPIA (South Africa) and NDPR (Nigeria), including data access, correction, and deletion requests.

Contact us at privacy@innkie.com to exercise these rights.

8. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies for essential site functionality and analytics. Non-essential cookies require your consent. You can adjust preferences in your browser or our cookie settings banner.

9. Security of Data

We implement encryption, access controls, and secure hosting. While we strive for full protection, no system is entirely immune from unauthorized access.

10. Children’s Privacy

iNNkie does not knowingly collect data from anyone under 13 (or 16 in the EU/EEA). If we learn of such collection, we delete it promptly.

11. Updates to This Policy

We may revise this Privacy Policy periodically. Material changes will be notified via the app or email. The latest version will always be available on this page.

12. Contact Information

For privacy inquiries, please email: privacy@innkie.com
